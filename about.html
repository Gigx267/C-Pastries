Tradition Meets Excellence

A Taste of Heritage, Crafted in Toronto

C Pastries began with a simple, heartfelt mission: to share the rich, vibrant flavors of authentic Nigerian smallchops with the Greater Toronto Area. Far from being just snacks, our pastries are a celebration of culture, family recipes, and the joy of shared moments.

Every single item, from the perfectly spiced Meat Pie to the airy, golden Puff Puff, is a testament to quality. We adhere strictly to traditional techniques while selecting only the finest, freshest ingredients. This dedication ensures an unparalleled, luxurious tasting experience that honors our heritage.
