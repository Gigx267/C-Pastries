Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

Do you offer catering for events?

Yes! We specialize in catering for events of all sizes. Contact us with your event details and we'll create a custom package for you.

How far in advance should I place my order?

For regular orders, we recommend placing them at least 24 hours in advance. For large orders or catering, please contact us at least 3-5 days ahead.

Do you offer delivery?

Yes, we offer delivery within the Greater Toronto Area. Delivery fees vary based on location and order size.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, debit, credit cards, and e-transfers for your convenience.

Are your products suitable for people with dietary restrictions?

Some of our products can be modified to accommodate dietary needs. Please contact us to discuss your specific requirements.

Do you have vegetarian options?

Yes, we offer several vegetarian options including vegetable samosas and spring rolls. Check our menu for more details.
